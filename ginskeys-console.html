03  FOH MIX NOTES▼
GENERAL
▸ Hard rock — guitars prominent, kick punchy
▸ Vocals clear above guitars at all times
▸ No click track · No tracks / playback
▸ Mono-compatible mix essential
PER INSTRUMENT
▸ Kick: tight low-mid, gate, transient shaper
▸ Guitar: +3dB @ 3–5kHz, cut @ 300Hz
▸ Bass: DI + amp blend, LP @ 5kHz
▸ Vocals: plate reverb 1.2s, chorus delay only
